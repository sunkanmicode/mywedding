Our Story

The Journey To Forever

This is a tale of ‘"HE" Choir director and "SHE" Choir member’. She joined the choir when I was the Music Director in Liveliest Model Parish RCCG. We developed a brother-sister relationship (as in... bro Lasun, sis Seun kind of church friendship....🤣). After one to two years in choir we went our ways but we kept the communication flowing. Nine years later we had our first date and she said yes with her quiet and lovely smile and the whirlwind romance began.


It has been a journey of God’s unwavering faithfulness and now, almost three years down the line, we can’t wait to tie the knot and start our happily ever after.
